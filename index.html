Epsilon Pi Tau Honor Society

Curry College is pleased to announce the establishment of the Delta Chi Chapter of the Epsilon Pi Tau Honor Society. Epsilon Pi Tau is an international honor society dedicated to the technology fields. Epsilon Pi Tau will recognize the academic excellence of Curry College students studying Information Technology or Software Development. To qualify for acceptance students must have completed 30 semester hours at Curry College and 16 semester hours in their major (IT or SD), have a GPA of 3.25 or higher, and be in the top 35% of the qualifying students.
